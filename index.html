Who am I?

Aïmane ALASSANE, is an aspiring IA Scientist convinced that the synergy between mathematics and artificial intelligence has the power to transform our daily lives. Student in 3rd year of IT expertise at Epitech Benin , he views data as revealing invisible truths.

Passionate about education, he experiments with innovative teaching methods — learning through play, immersive experiences, data-driven analysis — to rethink the way we learn.

"Revolutionary in thought, rigorous in execution." Inspired by Nelson Mandela — "I never lose: either I win, or I learn" — he moves forward with a continuous growth mentality.

His versatility is his driving force: it allows him to connect ideas, transform obstacles into opportunities and design solutions creative, intelligent and sustainable.
